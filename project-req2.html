Health care Modernization project, renovation of Jos Central hospital

Project Description

The Health Care Modernization project aims to renovate and upgrade the infrastructure and facilities of Jos Central Hospital, a prominent healthcare institution in the region. The project will focus on transforming the hospital into a state-of-the-art medical facility that provides high-quality healthcare services to the community. The renovation efforts will encompass various aspects of the hospital, including the physical infrastructure, medical equipment, technology systems, and patient care areas. The project will involve a comprehensive assessment of the existing hospital structure to identify areas that require improvement and modernization..
