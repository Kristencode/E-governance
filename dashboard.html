1. Personnel costs: N400 million

- This would cover salaries and benefits for healthcare workers such as doctors, nurses, and support staff.

2. Medical supplies and equipment: N200 million

- This would include expenses for purchasing medical equipment like surgical tools, diagnostic machines, and hospital beds, as well as supplies like medications, vaccines, and personal protective equipment (PPE).

3. Infrastructure and facility improvements: N100 million

- This could be used to fund renovations or expansion of existing healthcare facilities, or to build new ones. It could also cover costs related to upgrading technology systems, improving accessibility for patients with disabilities, or ensuring compliance with safety standards.

4. Health education and promotion: N50 million

- This would involve campaigns aimed at educating the public about healthy habits and preventing diseases. Examples could include distributing informational materials, organizing health fairs, or hosting seminars on topics like nutrition or stress management.

5. Research and development: N50 million

- This could go towards funding research studies on new treatments or therapies, or developing innovative technologies for use in healthcare.
